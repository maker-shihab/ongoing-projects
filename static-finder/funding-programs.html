Funding for fully inpatient long-term care facilities with an opening to the local community

The Free State of Bavaria promotes the nationwide and needs-based expansion of the nursing care structure so that people in need of care can remain at home in their familiar surroundings - in their local social environment - for as long as possible.

  • Region: Germany
  • Beneficiaries: Small Enterprises
  • Sectors: Agriculture, Forestry & Fishing + Food Production
  • Maximum amount: 5,000,000 €
  • Deadline: 31.12.2028
  • Funding Provider: Federal Ministry of Health
  • Managed by: Project Management Jülich (PtJ)
